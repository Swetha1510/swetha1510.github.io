About MPower

Mpower Resources is the place where client needs meet the right Talent. We are highly specialized in providing thoughtful and effective staffing solutions with unparalleled commitment to meet your needs. It's not just about what we do but how we do it is all that makes the difference. Integrity, Passion, Commitment and Seamless Speed are our core values. We strive to deliver superior value to our clients and consultants.

Attention to Details

At Mpower we believe in attention to detail, because we focus on getting things right first time, we care more for our clients and candidates as we want everyone to win and succeed. Mpower understands one error can ruin the credibility of the organisation and we recognise if we don’t care, why should other people? So getting things right first time and attention to detail are part of core competencies for all at Mpower.

Mentorship

The management at Mpower recognise that leadership and guidance is very important to success in nearly every field, and workplace mentoring is one way to provide personalized leadership for both new and tenured employees at our organisation. Skills-based Mentoring is part of on boarding to teach new executives the company ropes and to help them excel and grow as they continue their positions.

Efficiency

A team is defined as a group where individuals with similar skill sets, knowledge, specialization and expertise come together on a common platform to perform same or related tasks. We believe team work encourages employees to contribute as per their capabilities to reach to a common goal i.e. accomplish tasks within desired time frame. Our selection process looks for team players with right talent to meet the goals and improve efficiencies for the organisations.

Career Oriented

We understand our employees are ambitious and expect us to identify a clear-cut path towards their future growth. Our orientation program provides a clear sense of the company’s purpose, mission, value and goals and where an individual fits in the grand scheme of things. Our management programs are designed to drive retention and consultant loyalty.
Our motto “Fulfilment at work and fulfilment at home”.
